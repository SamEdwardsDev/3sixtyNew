We are specialist highway contractors with asphalt surfacing, patching and thermoplastic road marking capabilities already in house. Through the demand of our Client base and the market we are broadening our services to include further surface treatments and applications as listed below. We're still with you from design through to maintenance and beyond as we continue to deliver our customer focused Platinum services.

MMA

Cold applied road markings/ MMA Coloured surface paint MMA (Methyl Methacrylate) is an extremely durable product usually used at high stress traffic locations such as roundabouts, junctions and heavily trafficked roads. MMA provides a tough and an exceptional anti-slip surface on interior surfaces or exterior asphalt, resistant to mechanical stress and many chemicals including fuels, oils and lubricants.

HIGH FRICTION SURFACING

HFS (High friction surfacing) is a proven road surface treatment that increases skid resistance and reduces braking distance thereby reducing the potential for accidents. With a skid accident reduction of over 50% it saves lives and money. This is available in a range of colours to offer a visual demarcation as well as a high performing surface.

>

SPRAY

Spray painting lays down a perfect smooth high end finish every time, ideal for warehouses, garages, and other industrial floors and creates a finish with no streaks or imperfections. Spray road markings offer benefits in terms of maintenance compared to thermoplastic as the application is thinner meaning there is less cause for intrusive removal.

>

SPORT COURTS

We design custom sports courts to suit your needs. Specialising in multi sport courts, tennis courts and all weather sports surfaces. We refresh existing court surfaces and associated road markings.

SOFT PLAY

We install soft play surfaces, smooth clean cut rubber that is safety soft flooring suitable for climbing frame areas brilliant for safety and all fitted to regulations and guidelines and is environmentally friendly whilst being very versatile.

>

JOINT SEALING / CRACKS SEALING

This is a tried and tested method for early stage intervention to prevent further deterioration to any asphalt surface. This can be a 2 stage application for larger cracks or an overbanding solution for visible joints.

PRE FORMED THERMOPLASTIC

Provides a precise marking, with predefined shapes this eliminates the potential for potential issues during application. By utilising thermoplastic markings, making the project more efficient with less downtime

ANTI SKID OVERBANDING

Our hot applied method is revolutionary in terms of highway preservation, our Case Study offers further information. Safety is enhanced, grip is improved and stopping distances are reduced therefore reducing the probability of collisions and other accidents.

DECORATIVE SURFACES

We design and can print pretty much anything you wish, we have done everything from school badges at the entrance of schools to games and educational pictures that are bright, vibrant and uplifting. These surfaces can be achieved using several of our surface treatment applications.
